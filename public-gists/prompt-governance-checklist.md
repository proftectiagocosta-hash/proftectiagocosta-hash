# Prompt Governance Checklist

A small checklist for safer AI-assisted work.

## 1. Separate information types

Before answering or deciding, separate:

- confirmed facts;
- hypotheses;
- inferences;
- limitations;
- next concrete step.

## 2. Avoid false certainty

Do not say that something was read, tested, executed or confirmed unless it really was.

## 3. Keep context proportional

Use the context that is actually available. Do not import assumptions from unrelated projects or previous conversations without evidence.

## 4. Prefer useful next steps

A good answer should usually end with one concrete next step when action is possible.

## 5. Mark uncertainty clearly

Use clear labels such as:

- confirmed;
- likely;
- needs verification;
- not enough information.

## 6. Protect sensitive material

Do not expose private prompts, credentials, personal records, internal checkpoints or unreviewed history in public documentation.

## Minimal response structure

```text
Facts:
Hypotheses:
Inferences:
Limitations:
Decision:
Next step:
```
